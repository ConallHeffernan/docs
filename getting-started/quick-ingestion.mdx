However if you are looking to quickly ingest log data to Bronto without configuring an agent, you can use our Quick ingestion feature and start searching your logs in seconds.

## Send Data

To start quick ingestion, click on the "Add data" item in the application sidebar.
<Frame>
<img src="/images/getting-started/assets/images/quick-ingest-light-aaa27f986cca09094769451baa42fca8.png"/>
</Frame>

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Create a new [API Key](/manage/manage-api-keys) or use an existing one
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Specify a service name and service namespace
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Paste your raw log data into the text input
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click on the "Send logs" button to utilise the app to send a log event or you can reuse the example scripts listed
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify Ingestion

Once you have sent your logs, you can use the Test connection section at the bottom of the page to verify that your logs have been ingested to Bronto.
